Why Cyber Square

Cyber Square is the best online coding platform for kids that offers various online coding courses which will make kids future-ready. It helps kids to expose their creativity and acquire 21st-century skills.

Cyber Square is a learning platform for kids from age 4 - 17 to enhance coding skills and to shift from users of technology to creators of technology. The platform offers online courses for students, curriculum for CBSE and British schools to teach their IT subject and focuses on teaching Coding, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics and IoT.

Cyber Square, is an initiative by Baabte System Technologies Pvt Ltd, an EdTech company founded in 2010.In 2014, Cyber Square introduced at Habitat School Al Jerf, Ajman and they became the first school in the UAE to start teaching coding from Grade 1 as part of curriculum.

On 12 December 2019, Cyber Square set up the first Artificial Intelligence School lab for the first time in UAE, i.e. in Habitat School Al Tallah.Cyber Square is the first company in India to launch Artificial Intelligence aligned curriculum from Grade 2 for CBSE Schools which was inaugurated by Ms Debjani Ghosh, President, NASSCOM at the Digital Fest event of Dayapuram Residential School, Kozhikode Kerala.
